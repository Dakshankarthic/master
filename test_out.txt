# PGP APPLIED AI AND AGENTIC SYSTEMS - OFFICIAL PROGRAM DETAILS

- Campus Location: Gurugram, India (DLF Cyberpark).
- Total Program Fee: ₹22,65,000.
- Program Duration: 15 months (divided into 6 terms).
- Next Batch Starts: August 2026.
- Program Modes: 
  - Offline (On-Campus) for freshers or early career engineers.
  - Online Weekend Programme for working professionals to apply AI at their current job.

### ADMISSION PROCESS (4 STEPS)
1. Application Submission: Online academic records and professional experience.
2. Scholastic Technical Test: MU-BAAT or Scholastic Technical Test (GMAT/GRE/CAT also accepted).
3. Technical Interview: Interviewed by faculty and industry experts/mentors.
4. Admission Decision: Final decision via offer letter.

### FINANCES & INSTALLMENTS
- Total Program Fee: ₹22,65,000 (Inclusive of all tuition).
- Admission Offer Acceptance Fee: ₹75,000 (Non-refundable).
- Mid-Programme Installment: ₹10,95,000 (Due 3 months after program start).
- Scholarship: Merit-based and profile-based scholarships of up to 100% are available.
- EMI Option: Available via partner banks (HDFC, Bajaj Finserv). 12-month EMI is exactly ₹188,750 per month.
